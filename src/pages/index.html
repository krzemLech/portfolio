About me

I am a full-stack developer, focused on the front-end, but with an extensive Node.js experience and also a huge Python enthusiast. My favourite frameworks by far are: React at the front and Express at the backend, although recently I was captivated by Astro.js and Hono. My experience accounts for almost 4 years in enterprise. During this time I managed to create several applications, including storehouse management interface, financial apps and even a virtual machine rental application. I developed both front-end and back-end for these projects, depending on where my help was needed most. I feel comfortable with switching my development focus, as I practiced that a lot in my recent projects. Currently I am wroking for Codete.

My interests include programming, which I have been improving for the past 6 years, initially as a hobby and now as my day-time job. Work aside, I am also interested in calisthenics, cycling, startegic / table-top games and history. As an alumni of American Studies (i.a.) I speak fluent English and have quite an extensive experience in management, including a position of the Deputy Headmaster of a high school.
