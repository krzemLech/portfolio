About me

I am mid-level full-stack developer, focused on the front-end, but with an extensive Node.js experience and also a huge Python enthusiast. My favourite frameworks by far are: React at the front and Express at the backend, although recently I was captivated by Vue.js. My experience accounts for 2 years in enterprise, and during this time I managed to create several applications, including storehouse management interface, financial apps and even a virtual machine rental application. I created both front-end and back-end in these projects, depending on where my help was needed most. I feel comfortable with switching my development focus (front and backend of the same project and even developing several projects at one time), as I experienced that a lot recently in my softwarehouse. Currently I am wroking for Codete

My interests include programming, which I have been improving for the last 4 years, initially as a hobby and now as my day-time job. Aside from programming, I am also interested in calisthenics, cycling, startegic / table-top games and history. I speak fluent English and have quite an extensive experience in management.
